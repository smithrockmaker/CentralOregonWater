# What is this Acre-Foot?

The basic concept is incredibly straight forward. An acre-ft is 12" of water covering 1 acre. Conceptually straight forward but not at all helpful in understanding the water delivery system. 

Alternatively and acre-ft is equivalent to:   
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;1233.5 m$^3$   
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;43,560 ft$^3$
