## Central Oregon Water Issues:

In the years that we have been living rurally in Central Oregon questions about water have popped up continuously. What we can and can't do. What our water rights actually are and how they can or can't be used. How much water is delivered and how. How that water is measured. The list goes on and on.

Over the last couple of years (2024/2025) a group of patrons of COID have gathered to try to learn more about current and future irrigation water management in the context of the broader water management picture in the Deschutes Water Basin. There is an overwhelming about of data that I feel like I'm trying to keep track of it and when I focus on one issue I forget other parts of the picture. Again, that's why I am pushing to develop this reference.
