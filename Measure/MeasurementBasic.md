## The Basics

The United States Department of the Interior Bureau of Reclamation is the government agency most associated with the measurement of water in the open ditches that characterize older irrigation districts across the west. The [Water Measurement Manual (WMM)](https://www.usbr.gov/tsc/techreferences/mands/wmm/) is commonly referenced is many discussions I have been part of. For now I want to just define the various forms of weirs and note which are common here in CO.
